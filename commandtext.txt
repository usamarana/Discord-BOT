BOT Commands:
- !userinfo "tag the user" (or don't tag anyone to see own info)
- !serverinfo
- !botinfo

ADMIN ONLY COMMANDS:
- !join (joins voice chat)
- !leave (leaves voice chat)
- !message "message content" (will send the message to the specfic channel)
- !dm "tag the user" (or don't tag anyone to private message yourself)
- !delete "number of messages"
- !kick "tag the user"
